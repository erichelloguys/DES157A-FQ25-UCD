Design Template One: Conveyor Belt

Conceptual Changes

Template One is very simple. The idea is to roll the gallery of images across the screen like a conveyor belt either vertically and horizontally and as they approach the edge of the screen, I would use an observer or an event-listener to make the images disappearing to the edge more opaque, smaller, or disappear entirely depending on the difficulty of the design.

Design Revisions

This design, although different from one previously imagined where I would have a movie theater like setting with a primary focus of a single image and one thumbnail, would require some much more advanced techniques and execution to complete.

Interaction Changes

Interactions are yet to be added but the user is expected to be able to scroll, zoom, and hover to expand if necessary.

Concerns & Questions

The key is how to fade images in and out of any area without making it look unnatural or glitching and likely would require a lot of trial and error.
